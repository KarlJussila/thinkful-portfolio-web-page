About Me

In middle school, I picked up coding as a hobby. For a long time, I worked on projects focused on whatever piqued my interest. My early projects included a significant plugin for a Minecraft server, an application written in Electron to organize barrels for my family's winery, and some work on a biological imaging system used to study single cell DNA organization. I am driven to write code that provides intuitive and simple solutions to complex problems. Web development provides a way for me to share my appreciation of that with others. When I began coding, it was difficult to find others to work with on projects, but that is something I hope to do much more of in the near future.

Most of my interests revolve around coding, like game development and video games in general, but my other interests include
